# E-Finance
Departmental budget draft management platform

A platform dedicated to the Finance Department of Amikom University, Purwokerto. This website-based platform utilizes Media Composer to deliver stable and seamless performance. E-Finance is built using native PHP for easy future management.

We offer unlimited opportunities for programmers to develop this platform, and we encourage you to utilize it to your fullest potential for learning. For colleagues who wish to contribute or collaborate, please contact us by email at irfan.santiko@amikompurwokerto.ac.id.
